About the
Creator

Hello! My name is Juhi Raghavan and I created this website with one goal in mind: to help young girls gain real world skills that are often not taught in schools. I participated in the Global Youth Excellence & Leadership program (GYEL) where I learned about many global issues through the lens of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). SDG 5: Gender Equality especially resonated with me as I learned about all the inequalities that women face around the world. By starting small and teaching about life skills, I hope to equip girls with the confidence and tools they need to navigate everyday challenges, become independent, and step into leadership roles, no matter their background.
