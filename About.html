About the
Creator

Hi! I’m Juhi Raghavan, and I created this website to help young girls gain real-world skills that schools often overlook. Through the Global Youth Excellence & Leadership (GYEL) program, I explored global issues and was especially inspired by SDG 5: Gender Equality. Learning about the challenges women face worldwide motivated me to start small—by teaching life skills that build confidence, independence, and leadership. This is just the beginning, and I’m excited to keep growing and empowering more girls!
